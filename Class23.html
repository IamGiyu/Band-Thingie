Måneskin

Why is Måneskin called Måneskin?

Måneskin means Moonshine in Danish. The name was
inspired by bassist Victoria De Angelis' half Danish heritage.

Who are the members of Måneskin?

Damiano David (Lead vocalist)
Victoria De Angelis (Bass)
Ethan Torchio (Drum)
Thomas Raggi (Guitar)

When was Maneskin formed?

2016.

How did Måneskin form?

The bandmates first met each other as students of the same high
school in Monteverde Rome. The band asked their half Danish bassist
Victoria De Angelis to offer up some Danish words and soon enough Måneskin was born.

Who is the youngest in Måneskin?

Damiano is the eldest member of the band Måneskin, with Thomas the youngest being only 20.
